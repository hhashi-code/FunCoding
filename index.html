Khobar is a coastal city located in Eastern Province that overlooks the Persian Gulf. Due to its industrial development and access to beautiful beaches, there are numerous things to do in Al Khobar. The activities include visiting brilliant seashores, attractive parks, gorgeous skyscrapers, entertainment centers, and museums. The most appealing tourist destination in the city is Al Khobar Corniche, a long road parallel to the coastline. Visitors can explore the Corniche Mosque and Al Khobar Water Tower among the most popular attractions in the city.
